Our games

These are some of our last and more beloved games.

Terraria

Our favourite... and could be yours!

Terraria is a sandbox 2d adventure game with more than 3500 assets and more than 10 bosses. Explore among the 10 biomes to get weapons, tools, objects and more. Play in coop mode with your friends to slay all the final bosses or fight in pvp to know who is the best.

Among Us

Have a good time with your friends

Play this game online or local mode with 4 to 15 players. You have to prepare your spaceship for liftoff. But, beware: One or more players randomly elected are impostors seeking to kill the rest! When a player finds a dead body, they can report it, which pauses the game and leads to a discussion where people can chat to find out who The Impostors are.

Cuphead

Our last release, to enjoy singleplayer

On the fictional Inkwell Isles, Cuphead and his brother Mugman are two fun-loving kids who live under the watchful eye of Elder Kettle. Against the elder's warnings, the brothers enter the Devil's Casino and begin playing craps. When they go on a winning streak, the Devil himself offers to raise the stakes.
