Brawl Stars

About Brawl Stars

This video game for iOS and Android devices is based on battles between two teams composed of three characters, each of them remotely managed by a user. The different eligible fighters or brawlers have a unique set of weapons and unique weaponry and abilities, which focus their actions towards specific tasks in pursuit of a final goal : to defeat the opposing gang.

This is the main type of game, but you will also you will also find other game modes. Among them, you have a rotating game mode in which you have to get 10 gems and hold them for 15 seconds before the opposing team , a handball game full of cartoon violence, a safe robbery in a duel to see who can blow up the other team's box and even a twist on the Battle Royale trend with a small map populated by populated by 10 players.

The game features more than 20 characters called Brawlers, and are divided between eight main ones and several categories of specials. Each of these characters has a main and secondary skills, so one of the secrets is to choose the one that best suits you.
