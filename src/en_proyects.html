Our games

Here you can check out all of our titles, created by our team with lots of effort and exitement for you, players. There are all the genres, from 2d plataforming to fast-paced multiplayers, and also Sandbox titles. We hope you find some of your liking and that you can enjoy playing it as much as we developing it!

Terraria

Our favourite... and could be yours!

Terraria is a sandbox 2d adventure game with more than 3500 assets and more than 10 bosses. Explore among the 10 biomes to get weapons, tools, objects and more. Play in coop mode with your friends to slay all the final bosses or fight in pvp to know who is the best.

Cuphead

Our last release, to enjoy singleplayer

On the fictional Inkwell Isles, Cuphead and his brother Mugman are two fun-loving kids who live under the watchful eye of Elder Kettle. Against the elder's warnings, the brothers enter the Devil's Casino and begin playing craps. When they go on a winning streak, the Devil himself offers to raise the stakes.

Among Us

Have a good time with your friends

Play this game online or local mode with 4 to 15 players. You have to prepare your spaceship for liftoff. But, beware: One or more players randomly elected are impostors seeking to kill the rest! When a player finds a dead body, they can report it, which pauses the game and leads to a discussion where people can chat to find out who The Impostors are.

Hollow Knight

A solo adventure in a extrange world

Under the deteriorated Petropolis city there's an ancient crumbling reign. Lots are atracted by life under the surface and go searching wealth, glory or answers to old enigmas. Hollow Knight is a 2D classic adventure localizated in a vast interconnected world. Explore dangerous caverns, ancient cities and deadly wastelands.

Fall Guys

The trendy adventure multiplayer

Fall Guys: Ultimate Knockout pits hordes of online contenders in a crazy, round-by-round showdown that unfolds round after round amidst growing chaos until only a single victor remains. Overcome bizarre obstacles, fight your way through unruly competitors and beat the inflexible laws of physics on your bumpy road to greatness.

Brawl Stars

If you are a mobile gamer

Brawl Stars is a real time battle game, in which two teams of three characters each will have to face each other in a scenario full of obstacles and elements with which they can interact. Your goal: to teach the opposing team a good lesson.
