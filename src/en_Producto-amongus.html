Among Us

About Among Us

Among Us is a game very similar to the classic "The Killer" game we have played sometimes in cards, but taken to the world of video games. You are on a spaceship, and two impostors who are "among us", hence the title, have to kill the other eight crew members before they are discovered.

So, it is an online multiplayer game that can be played by 4 to 10 people. When you log in, two of them are assigned the role of impostors, and only they know that they have been selected for it. The rest will have no idea who among the participants is a normal player or an impostor.

The impostors will have to assassinate the rest of the participants without being discovered, being able to sabotage the ship to cause chaos so that, amidst the confusion, they can try to act. In the meantime, the rest of the ship members have to try to win by completing all the tasks tasks assigned to them, or by discovering and ejecting the impostor out of the ship into outer space before he kills them all.
